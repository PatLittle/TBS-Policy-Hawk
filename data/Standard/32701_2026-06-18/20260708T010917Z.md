# Design Standard for the Federal Identity Program
This standard explains how to apply the official symbols of the Government of Canada in all media, platforms, products, material, equipment and real property in Canada and abroad.
- This standard takes effect on November 19, 2021, and applies to departments that are subject to the [Policy on Communications and Federal Identity](https://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/pol/doc-eng.aspx?id=30683) and its associated instruments.
This standard replaces the following volumes in the Federal Identity Program Manual:
1.1 Design: Symbols, typography, signatures, colour (October 1990)
5.1 Advertising: Corporate identity guidelines (March 1989)
5.2 Published material: Corporate identity guidelines (July 1990)
