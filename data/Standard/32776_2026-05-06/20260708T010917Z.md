# Inventory of data and metadata reference standards
This page provides links to Government of Canada data and metadata reference standards issued under Appendix K of the [Directive on Service and Digital](https://www.tbs-sct.canada.ca/pol/doc-eng.aspx?id=32601).
The standards are in alphabetical order by topic. Additional standards are being developed and will be published here when they are ready. For more information about data and metadata reference standards, visit the [About data and metadata reference standards](https://www.canada.ca/en/government/system/digital-government/digital-government-innovations/enabling-interoperability/about-data-metadata-reference-standards.html) page.
## Reference standards
### [Business number](https://www.canada.ca/en/government/system/digital-government/digital-government-innovations/enabling-interoperability/gc-enterprise-data-reference-standards/data-reference-standard-business-number.html)
Requires the use of business numbers in all transactions between businesses and the Government of Canada.
Effective date: 2024-05-07
Stewards: Canada Revenue Agency, Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada
### [Canadian provinces and territories](https://www.canada.ca/en/government/system/digital-government/digital-government-innovations/enabling-interoperability/gc-enterprise-data-reference-standards/data-reference-standard-canadian-provinces-territories.html)
Lists the names and codes that must be used when describing and sharing data on Canadian provinces and territories.
Effective date: 2023-10-02
Stewards: Statistics Canada and Natural Resources Canada
Machine-readable formats: [CSV](https://open.canada.ca/data/en/dataset/cd8fad92-b276-4250-972f-2d6c40ca04fa/resource/4cb827a6-a313-4d97-a66b-26fe23984931) | [JSON](https://open.canada.ca/data/en/dataset/cd8fad92-b276-4250-972f-2d6c40ca04fa/resource/86c1f4f2-b5f7-4448-84e7-1a7fc4aac17c)
