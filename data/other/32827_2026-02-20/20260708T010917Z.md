# Real Property Policy Notices
To enhance transparency for Canadian citizens and industry, beginning in 2025, the Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat began issuing Real Property Policy Notices (RPPNs).
RPPNs are official communications issued by the Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat’s Office of the Comptroller General. These notices provide updates, interpretation and guidance related to the management of federal real property, and support government departments and agencies in awareness and compliance with regulations and [Treasury Board policy instruments](https://www.tbs-sct.canada.ca/pol/topic-sujet-eng.aspx?ta=50).
