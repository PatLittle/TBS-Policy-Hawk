# Union of Canadian Correctional Officers (UCCO) – Change to Membership Dues

Date: August 19, 2024

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre.

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Union of Canadian Correctional Officers (UCCO) has changed the rate of its monthly membership dues, effective September 1, 2024 for two of its Institutions; Donnacona Institution in Donnacona, QC, and the Archambault Institution (minimum security) in Ste-Anne-des-Plaines, QC.

Membership dues are decreasing for all members of the above institutions as follows:

| Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- |
| 2.03% | 1.95% |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Employees should direct any questions on this matter to the Union of Canadian Correctional Officers (UCCO) at [ucco-sacc@csn.qc.ca](mailto:ucco-sacc@csn.qc.ca).

Original signed by

Wendy Bennen Director, Compensation Policy and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
