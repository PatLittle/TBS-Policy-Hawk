# Rates of Pay for Excluded Employees of the CX Group

Date: April 11, 2018

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

I am pleased to announce the revised rates of pay as approved by the Treasury Board of Canada for excluded positions in the Correctional Services (CX) Supervisory Group.

The attached appendix provides the rates of pay for the excluded positions.

The new [rates of pay](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.html#Toc476385500) are available on the TBS Website.

All questions should be directed to your departmental corporate labour relations or corporate compensation official who, if required, will direct their questions to the following email address: [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Original signed by Zia Proulx Senior Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
