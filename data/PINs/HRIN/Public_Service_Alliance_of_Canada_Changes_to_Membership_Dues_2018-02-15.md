# Public Service Alliance of Canada - Changes to Membership Dues

Date: February 15, 2018

To: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this bulletin is to inform you that the members of the National Resources Union (NRU) have agreed to merge with the Union of Canadian Transportation Employees (UCTE). All of the members will now be under the Union of Canadian Transportation Employees (UCTE). The merger took effect on October 1st, 2017.

Please note that all members affected by this action will be subject to the existing UCTE rate of 0.7862% as of October 1, 2017.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to their Component, the UCTE, at (613) 238-4003 or at [their website](http://www.ucte.ca/contact-us/).

Original signed by

Terry Findlay A/Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
