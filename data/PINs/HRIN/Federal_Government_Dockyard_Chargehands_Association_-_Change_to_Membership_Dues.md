# Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association – Change to Membership Dues

August 5, 2020

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

Subject: Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association – Change to Membership Dues

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association (FGDCA) has changed the monthly membership dues, for the Ships Repair (All Chargehands and Production Supervisor Employees Located on the East Coast) (SR (C)) group effective April 1, 2020.

Membership dues are decreasing as follows:

| Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- |
| $75.00 | $40.00 |

This adjustment will be made automatically.

Employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association.

Original signed by

Zia Proulx Senior Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
