# Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) Union of Health and Environment Workers - Changes to Membership Dues

Date: December 1, 2022

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective January 1, 2023.

On that date, the Union of Health and Environment Workers component’s portion of their dues’ rate will increase in dues from 0.4920% to 0.4921% per member per month.

The required adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the [Public Service Alliance of Canada](https://psacunion.ca/contact-us).

Original signed by

Wendy Braithwaite Director, Compensation Policy, Interpretations, and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
