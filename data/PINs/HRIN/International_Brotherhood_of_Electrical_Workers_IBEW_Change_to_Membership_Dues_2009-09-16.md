Date: September 16, 2009

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW) has advised us of a change to membership dues effective September 1, 2009.

The following chart includes the exact deduction amounts:

| Classification | Current Rates | New Rates |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EL-1 | $ 63.55 | $ 64.32 |
| EL-2 | $ 74.14 | $ 75.07 |
| EL-3 | $ 81.00 | $ 82.04 |
| EL-4 | $ 85.84 | $ 86.95 |
| EL-5 | $ 93.90 | $ 95.13 |
| EL-6 | $102.44 | $103.80 |
| EL-7 | $110.61 | $112.09 |
| EL-8 | $117.91 | $119.50 |
| EL-9 | $125.84 | $127.55 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee inquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to IBEW.

Marc Thibodeau Director CPA Compensation Operations Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
