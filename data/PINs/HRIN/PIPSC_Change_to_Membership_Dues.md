# PIPSC - Change to Membership Dues

Date: December 12, 2007

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Professional Institute of the Public Service of Canada (PIPSC) has advised us of membership dues rate changes, taking effect on January 1, 2008.

The PIPSC membership dues will increase from $47.00 to $55.56.

These adjustments will be made in the pay system automatically by Public Works and Government Services Canada (PWGSC). No departmental input will be required.

Please note that this rate change applies to employees in the Applied Science and Patent Examination (SP); Audit, Commerce and Purchasing (AV); Computer Systems Administration (CS); Health Services (SH) Research (RE); and Architecture, Engineering and Land Survey (NR ) groups who are currently paying PIPSC dues.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations Managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to PIPSC.

Original signed by

Sylvie Joseph Acting Director, Project Management, Internal Service Transformation and Pay Administration Labour Relations and Compensation Operation
