Date: September 11, 2014

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Association of Professional Employees (CAPE) has advised us of a change in membership dues effective September 1, 2014.

Membership dues will increase as follows:

| Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- |
| $43.00 | $48.00 |

This adjustment will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding this change to membership dues are to be directed to the Canadian Association of Professional Employees.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation official.

Original Signed by

Virginie Emiel-Wildhaber A/Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officers
