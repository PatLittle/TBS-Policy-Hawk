Date: December 16, 2011

To: Directors and Chiefs of Labour; Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform departments that the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of changes in membership dues effective January 1, 2012.

The following is a list of changes by Component:

Agriculture Component: An increase from 0.5368% to 0.6951%.

Government Services Union: An increase from 0.5605% to 0.5610%.

Union of Environment Workers: A decrease from 0.6264% to 0.5750%.

Union of Veterans’ Affairs Employees: An increase from 0.5747 % to 0.5781%.

Union of Canadian Transportation Employees: An increase from 0.7831% to 0.7862%.

Natural Resources Union: An increase from 0.715% to 0.77%.

Union of Solicitor General Employees: An increase from 0.6845% to 0.6873%.

Union of National Employees (formerly the National Component): An increase from 0.4979% to 0.5433%.

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Any employee enquiries regarding these changes to membership dues are to be directed to the employee’s respective PSAC component.

Questions about this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation officer.

Original signed by

Kevin Marchand A/Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
