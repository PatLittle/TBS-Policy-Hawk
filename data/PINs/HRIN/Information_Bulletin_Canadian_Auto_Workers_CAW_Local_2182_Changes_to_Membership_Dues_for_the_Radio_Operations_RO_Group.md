Date: December 7, 2012

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Auto Workers Local 2182 have advised us of changes in membership dues effective January 1, 2013 and May 1, 2013.

Note: The new rates were sent to departments electronically and will not be posted to the website.

These adjustments will be made manually at the departmental level. Please disseminate this notice to your compensation advisors.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the Canadian Auto Workers Local 2182.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental corporate compensation official.

Kevin Marchand Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
