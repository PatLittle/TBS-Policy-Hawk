# Public Service Alliance of Canada – Increase to membership dues based on the recent salary increase

Date: October 27th, 2023

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and the Public Service Pay Centre

The Public Service alliance of Canada has informed us of an increase in membership dues effective November 1st, 2023 to reflect the recent salary increase for the following groups;

- Program and Administrative Services (PA)
- Technical Services (TC)
- Operational Services (SV)
- Education and Library Science (EB)

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the [Public Service Alliance of Canada](https://psacunion.ca/contact-us).

Original signed by

Wendy Braithwaite Director, Compensation Policy, Interpretations, and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
