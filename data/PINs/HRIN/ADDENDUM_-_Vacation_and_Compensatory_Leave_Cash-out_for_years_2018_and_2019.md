# ADDENDUM - Vacation and Compensatory Leave Cash-out for years 2018 and 2019

Date: March 22, 2018

To: Transport Canada, Transportation Safety Board of Canada, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

This is an addendum to the information bulletin issued on February 23, 2018 advising departments and agencies of the core public administration (CPA) of the Treasury Board Secretariat's decision to postpone the automatic cash-out of vacation and compensatory leave for 2018 and 2019, unless requested by the employee.

The bulletin issued on February 23, 2018 listed employees in the Aircraft Operations (AO) group as an exception.

The Canadian Federal Pilots Association (CFPA) has since asked that members of the AO group be included in this measure. The Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat has agreed to this request in light of the ongoing issues with the Phoenix pay system.

Please inform all concerned employees within your respective departments about this change within best possible delays.

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required will direct their questions to the Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management group at the following email address: [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).
