# The Canadian Merchant Service Guild (CMSG) – Changes to Membership Dues

Date: March 1, 2022

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform departments that the Canadian Merchant Service Guild (CMSG) has advised us of changes in the monthly membership dues, for Ships’ Officers (SO) effective April 1, 2022.

The monthly membership dues will increase from $107.50 to $111.16.

The monthly membership dues for the Coast Guard College Cadets (SO-MAO-00) will increase from $11.90 to $12.30.

Any employee inquiries, regarding this membership dues change, are to be directed to CMSG, [cmsg@ottawa-email.com](mailto:cmsg@ottawa-email.com).

Original signed by

Angelo Gatto A/Director Compensation Policy, Interpretations and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
