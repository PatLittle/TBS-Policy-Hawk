DATE: May 13, 2010

TO: Heads, Compensation Heads, Labour Relations

The Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of changes in membership dues effective July 1, 2010.

These changes in membership dues apply to the Program and Administrative Services group, as a result of the salary increase effective June 21, 2010 and the Education and Library Science group resulting from the salary increase effective July 1, 2010.

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Compensation Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to PSAC.

Marc Thibodeau Director Compensation Operations Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
