Date: May 26, 2014

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations; Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada has informed us of a change in monthly membership dues effective June 1, 2014.

This change in membership dues applies to the Operational Services Group.

This adjustment will be made automatically.

Employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the Public Service Alliance of Canada.

Kevin Marchand Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
