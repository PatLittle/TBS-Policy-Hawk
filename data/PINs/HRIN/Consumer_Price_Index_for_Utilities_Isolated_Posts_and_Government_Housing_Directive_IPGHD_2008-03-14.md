March 14, 2008

To: Functional Heads of Human Resources, Finance and Administration

The purpose of this notice is to provide departments and agencies with the revised Consumer Price Index (CPI) for Utilities.

Statistics Canada, now using 2002 as the base year, reports the January 2008 CPI for Utilities as 127.3. The IPGHD, however, uses 1991 as the base year. It was necessary, therefore, to re-base the January 2008 CPI to 1991. To do this, we applied the following formula:

127.3 (2008 CPI for Utilities) ÷ 0.719 (conversion factor) = 177.05

The index of 177.05 should be used in the calculation of utilities charges as per Section 6.14 of the Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive.

Please ensure that it is provided to those responsible for calculating the charges.

If you have any questions, please contact Jo-Anne Tremblay on (613) 952-3202.

Claudia Zovatto Senior Director Excluded Groups and Administrative Policies Labour Relations and Compensation Operations
