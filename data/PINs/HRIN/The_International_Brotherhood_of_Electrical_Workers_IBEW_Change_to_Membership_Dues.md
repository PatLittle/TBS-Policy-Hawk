# The International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW) – Change to Membership Dues

Date: October 7, 2024

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW) has changed the rate of its monthly membership dues, effective November 1, 2024.

Membership dues are decreasing for all members of the Electronics (EL) group as follows:

| Level | Current rate | New rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EL-01 | $96.46 | $95.40 |
| EL-02 | $111.56 | $109.80 |
| EL-03 | $121.32 | $119.40 |
| EL-04 | $128.22 | $126.16 |
| EL-05 | $139.70 | $137.40 |
| EL-06 | $151.88 | $149.36 |
| EL-07 | $163.52 | $160.76 |
| EL-08 | $173.92 | $170.96 |
| EL-09 | $185.22 | $182.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Employees should direct any questions on this matter to the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW) at [office@ibew2228.ca](mailto:office@ibew2228.ca).

Original signed by

Wendy Bennen Director, Compensation Policy and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
