# Changes to Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) bi-weekly default union dues deduction rate

Date: December 1, 2022

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform departments that the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has changed its bi-weekly default union dues deduction rate for new PSAC members effective March 1, 2023.

Details follow:

| Status | Current bi-weekly default union dues deduction rate | New bi-weekly default union dues deduction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Part-Time | $20.00 | $12.50 |
| Full-Time | $20.00 | $35.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required on your part.

Employees should direct any questions on this matter to [Public Service Alliance of Canada](http://psacunion.ca/contact-us).

Original signed by

Wendy Braithwaite Director, Compensation Policy, Interpretations, and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
