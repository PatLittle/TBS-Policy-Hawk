Date: August 19, 2008

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Canadian Military Colleges Faculty Association (CMCFA) has advised us of membership dues rate changes, for the University Teaching (UT) Group, that took effect on July 1, 2008 as follows:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| UT-1 | $42.50 | $44.40 |
| UT-2 | $51.20 | $54.30 |
| UT-3 | $61.30 | $65.60 |
| UT-4 | $73.00 | $77.90 |

Arrears for the months of July and August 2008 will be collected from members in September 2008. The new rates will be reflected in the pay system in October 2008.

These adjustments will be made automatically by Public Works and Government Services Canada (PWGSC). No department input will be required.

Please note that these rate changes apply to members of the UT Group in bargaining unit designator (BUD) 22700 who are currently paying CMCFA dues.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations Managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee inquiries, regarding these membership dues changes, are to be directed to CMCFA.

Sylvie Joseph Acting Director, Project Management, Internal Service Transformation and Pay Administration Labour Relations and Compensation Operations
