# Annual Update to the Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive - Appendices B, C, and I

Date: August 1, 2017

To: Functional Heads of Administration, Human Resources and Financial Service

CC: Chairs and Executive Directors of Northern Federal Regional Councils, Departmental Designated Coordinators for the IPGHD, Committee members of the NJC IPGHD (for information)

The purpose of this notice is to provide you with the approved updates to the NJC IPGHD. The changes in Appendices B, C, and I will be effective August 1, 2017. Please distribute this notice and the amended appendices of the National Joint Council (NJC) Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive (IPGHD) as quickly as possible to persons at your headquarters and regional offices who are involved in the administration of the allowances.

- [Appendix B – Rate for the Environment Allowance](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/d4/v237/s625/en#s625-tc-tm)
- [Appendix C – Rate for the Living Cost Differential](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/d4/v237/s626/en)
- [Appendix I – Charge for Meals or Rations](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/d4/v237/s632/en#s632-tc-tm)

Details regarding the methodology can be found in the [Guide to National Joint Council Rates and Allowances](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/doc.php?did=117%20&lang=en).

Any retroactive payments resulting from the above changes are to be administered in the same manner as retroactive increases to employees’ pay.

Any questions from employees and regional staff should be directed to the appropriate [Designated Departmental IPGHD Coordinators](http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/psm-fpfm/pay-remuneration/other-autres/ddhc-cmdl-eng.asp) who, as required, can contact the appropriate officers at the Treasury Board Secretariat.

Claudia Zovatto Senior Director, Union Engagement and NJC Support Compensation and Labour Relations Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
