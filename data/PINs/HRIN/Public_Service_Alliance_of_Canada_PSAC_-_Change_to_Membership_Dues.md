Date: November 25, 2013

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of a change in monthly membership dues effective December 1, 2013.

This change in membership dues applies to the Technical Services Group (TC), as a result of a salary increase.

This adjustment will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC).

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Kevin Marchand Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
