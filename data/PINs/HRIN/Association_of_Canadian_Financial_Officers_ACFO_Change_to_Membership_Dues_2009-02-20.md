February 20, 2009

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO) has advised us of a change to membership dues, taking effect on July 1, 2009 as follows:

| Classification | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| All FI members | $35.00 | $40.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to ACFO.

Original signed by

Sylvie Joseph Director, Project Management, Internal Service Transformation and Pay Administration Labour Relations and Compensation Operations
