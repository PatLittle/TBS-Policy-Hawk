# The International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers: Change to Membership Dues

Date: July 7, 2025

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW) is changing the rate of its monthly membership dues, effective September 1, 2025.

Membership dues are increasing for all members of the Electronics (EL) group as follows:

| Level | Current rate | New rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EL-01 | $95.40 | $96.46 |
| EL-02 | $109.80 | $111.56 |
| EL-03 | $119.40 | $121.32 |
| EL-04 | $126.16 | $128.22 |
| EL-05 | $137.40 | $139.70 |
| EL-06 | $149.36 | $151.88 |
| EL-07 | $160.76 | $163.52 |
| EL-08 | $170.96 | $173.92 |
| EL-09 | $182.00 | $185.22 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Employees should direct any questions on this matter to the IBEW at [office@ibew2228.ca](mailto:office@ibew2228.ca).

Original signed by

Wendy Bennen Director, Compensation Policy and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
