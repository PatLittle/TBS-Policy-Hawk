# The Canadian Association of Professional Employees - Change to membership dues

Date: July 12, 2023 To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and the Public Service Pay Centre

The Canadian Association of Professional Employees (CAPE) has informed us of a change in monthly membership dues effective November 1, 2023.

Details follow:

| Frequency | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly | $ 48.00 | $ 57.10 |
| Bi-Weekly | $ 24.00 | $ 28.55 |

This change will be made automatically; no action is required.

Employees should direct any questions on this matter to [general@acep-cape.ca.](mailto:general@acep-cape.ca)

Original signed by

Wendy Braithwaite Director, Compensation Policy, Interpretations, and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
