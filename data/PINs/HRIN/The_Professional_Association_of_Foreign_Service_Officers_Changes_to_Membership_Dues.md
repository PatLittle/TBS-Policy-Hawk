# The Professional Association of Foreign Service Officers - Changes to Membership Dues

Date: February 4, 2014

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The purpose of this bulletin is to inform you that the Professional Association of Foreign Service Officers has advised us of changes in monthly membership dues effective April 1, 2014.

Membership dues will increase as follows:

| Classification | Current Rates | New Rates |
| --- | --- | --- |
| FS-01 | $55.00 | $58.00 |
| FS-02 | $73.00 | $86.00 |
| FS-03 | $91.00 | $96.00 |
| FS-04 | $99.00 | $108.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the Professional Association of Foreign Service Officers.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Original signed by:

Kevin Marchand Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
