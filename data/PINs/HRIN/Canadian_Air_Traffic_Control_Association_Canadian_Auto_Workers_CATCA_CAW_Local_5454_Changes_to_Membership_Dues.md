Date: October 2, 2013

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Air Traffic Control Association, Canadian Auto Workers (CATCA, CAW) Local 5454 has informed us of changes in membership dues effective November 1, 2013.

The new dues rates are reflected in the attached document, which will not be posted with this information bulletin.

These adjustments will be made manually by the department. Please disseminate this notice to your compensation advisors.

Any employee enquiries regarding these changes to membership dues are to be directed to the Canadian Air Traffic Control Association, Canadian Auto Workers (CATCA, CAW) Local 5454.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Kevin Marchand Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
