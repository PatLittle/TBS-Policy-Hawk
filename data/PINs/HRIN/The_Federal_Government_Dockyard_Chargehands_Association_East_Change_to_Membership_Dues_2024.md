# The Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association (East) – Change to Membership Dues (2024)

Date: March 6, 2024

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre.

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association (FGDCA - EAST) has changed the rate of its monthly membership dues, effective July 1, 2024.

| Frequency | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly | $50.00 | $100.00 |
| Bi-Weekly | $25.00 | $50.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Employees should direct any questions on this matter to the Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association (East) at [fgdcapresident@gmail.com](mailto:fgdcapresident@gmail.com).

Original signed by:

Wendy Braithwaite Director, Compensation Policy and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
