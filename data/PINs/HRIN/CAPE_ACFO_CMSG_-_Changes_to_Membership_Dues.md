# Changes to Membership Dues to the Canadian Association of Professional Employees (CAPE), the Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO), and the Canadian Merchant Service Guild (CMSG)

Date: February 14, 2025

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre.

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that membership dues are increasing for all members covered under a collective agreement with the above mentioned Bargaining Agents.

|  | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly | $48.00 | $53.00 |

|  | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly | $71.90 | $73.18 |

|  | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly (SO) | $12.78 | $13.10 |
| Monthly (CCG) | $115.50 | $118.28 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to their respective Bargaining Agent.

Original signed by

Wendy Bennen Director, Compensation Policy and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
