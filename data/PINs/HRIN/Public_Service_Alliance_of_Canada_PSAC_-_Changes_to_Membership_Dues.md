# Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) - Changes to Membership Dues

Date: August 11, 2022

To: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation Public Service Pay Centre, and Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective September 1, 2022.

There will be an increase in dues from 0.9557% plus $1.00 per month per member to 0.9593% plus $1.00 per month per member for all employees covered by a collective agreement with the PSAC.

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the [Public Service Alliance of Canada](http://psacunion.ca/contact-us).
