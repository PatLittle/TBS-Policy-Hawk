# Rates of Pay and Terms and Conditions of Employment Modifications for Excluded Employees of the Program Administration Mediation Conciliation Sub Group, Sector 4

Date: July 9, 2015

To: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

I am pleased to announce the revised rates of pay as well as modifications to the Terms and Conditions of Employment for certain excluded employees, as approved by the Treasury Board of Canada, for the following group: Program Administration Mediation Conciliation Sub Group, Sector 4.

## Rates of Pay

The rates of pay applicable to these excluded employee groups can be found online at: [Rates of Pay for Certain Excluded and Unrepresented Employees](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.html).

The attached [Appendix A](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/information-notice/rates-pay-terms-conditions-employment-modifications-excluded-employees-program-administration-mediation-conciliation-sub-group.html#appA) report the rates of pay for those excluded positions.

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required will direct their questions to the Compensation and Labour Relations group at the following email address: [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

### Pay Notes

1. A PM-MCO-04 employee not yet at the salary range maximum shall have his or her rate of pay increased:
1. On April 1, 2013, to a rate within the "A" performance pay range which is one percent (1.0%) higher than his or her former rate of pay.
2. On April 1, 2014, to a rate within the "B" performance pay range which is zero point five percent (0.50%) higher than his or her former rate of pay.
3. On April 1, 2015, to a rate within the "C" performance pay range which is zero point five percent (0.50%) higher than his or her former rate of pay.

2. Round to nearest $100.
3. Approval Date: June 18, 2015
