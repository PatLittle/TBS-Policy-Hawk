# Rates of Pay and Terms and Conditions of Employment Modifications for Excluded Employees of Certain Specific Occupational Groups

Date: December 3, 2013

To: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

I am pleased to announce the revised rates of pay as well as modifications to the Terms and Conditions of Employment for certain excluded employees, as approved by the Treasury Board of Canada, for the following groups: Aircraft Operations (AO), Financial Management (FI), Technical Services (TC), Correctional Services (CX) and the Meteorology group of the Applied Science and Patent Examination (SP).

## Rates of Pay

The rates of pay applicable to these excluded employee groups correspond to those found in their relevant collective agreement and can be found online at: [Rates of Pay for the Public Service of Canada](http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/pubs_pol/hrpubs/coll_agre/rates-taux-eng.asp).

The rates of pay for those excluded positions for which different rates are listed compared to the collective agreement can be found online for the [Aircraft Operations (AO)](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.htm), [Financial Management (FI)](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.htm), Technical Services (TC) – [Primary Products Inspection Group (PI](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.htm)), [Correctional Services (CX)](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.htm) and the [Meteorology Group](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.htm) (MT) of the Applied Science and Patent Examination (SP).

## Severance Termination

Severance pay eligibility for voluntary separation (retirement and resignation) is discontinued as of the date indicated in the relevant collective agreement for all the groups listed above.

- The AO group: The estimate on years of continuous employment is to be provided no later than three (3) months from September 27, 2013, (the date of the approved modifications) that is, no later than December 27, 2013.
- The FI group: The estimate on years of continuous employment is to be provided no later than three (3) months from September 30, 2013, (the date of the approved modifications) that is, no later than December 30, 2013.
- The TC group: The estimate on years of continuous employment is to be provided no later than three (3) months from October 9, 2013, (the date of the approved modifications) that is, no later than January 9, 2014.
- The CX group: The estimate on years of continuous employment is to be provided no later than three (3) months from October 24, 2013, (the date of the approved modifications) that is, no later than January 24, 2014.
- The MT-08 group: The estimate on years of continuous employment is to be provided no later than three (3) months from September 10, 2013, (the date of the approved modifications) that is, no later than December 10, 2013.
- The AO group: Employees are to select their option within six (6) months from September 27, 2013, which is by March 26, 2014 inclusively.
- The FI group: Employees are to select their option within six (6) months from September 30, 2013, which is by March 29, 2014 inclusively.
- The TC group: Employees are to select their option within six (6) months from October 9, 2013, which is by April 8, 2014 inclusively.
- The CX group: Employees are to select their option within six (6) months from October 24, 2013, which is by April 23, 2014 inclusively.
- The MT-08 group: Employees are to select their option within six (6) months from September 10, 2013, which is by March 9, 2014 inclusively.

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required will direct their questions to the Compensation and Labour Relations group at the following email address: [Contact Interpretations by E-mail Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).
