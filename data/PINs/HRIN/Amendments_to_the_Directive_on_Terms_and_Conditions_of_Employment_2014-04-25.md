Date: April 25, 2014

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to advise you that the Directive on Terms and Conditions of Employment has been amended effective April 1, 2014.

Changes to certain sections of the [Appendix to the Directive](http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/pol/doc-eng.aspx?id=15772#appA) were necessary for the implementation of the Public Works and Government Services Canada Pay Modernization Project.

Key features are payment in arrears, the financial authority for a one-time transition payment and revising the provisions for direct deposit. Other changes were made to update the language of the directive to correspond to these new policy changes.

The following sections were amended:

- 10. Biweekly pay
- 12. Direct deposit
- 13. Release of regular salary and supplementary payments
- 16. Standard of timeliness
- 17. Emergency salary advance

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required will direct their questions to the Compensation and Labour Relations group at the following email address: [Contact Interpretations by E-mail Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Kevin Marchand Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
