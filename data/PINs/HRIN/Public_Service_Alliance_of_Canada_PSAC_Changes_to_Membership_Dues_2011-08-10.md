Date: August 10, 2011

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform departments that the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of changes in membership dues effective September 1, 2011.

These changes in membership dues apply to the following groups:

Program and Administrative Services (PA), as a result of salary increases reflected in the collective agreement signed on March 1, 2011; and

Operational Services (SV), as a result of salary increases reflected in the collective agreement signed on April 6, 2011.

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to PSAC.

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required, will direct their questions to the following e-mail address: [Contact Interpretations by E-mail Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Original signed by

Marc Thibodeau Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
