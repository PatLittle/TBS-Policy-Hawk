# Association of Canadian Financial Officers – Change to Membership Dues

Date: March 27, 2023

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and the Public Service Pay Centre

The Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO) has advised us of changes in monthly membership dues effective April 1, 2023.

The monthly membership dues will increase from $65.42 to $69.54. This is applicable to all employees represented by ACFO, including employees in the FI, AU and AS groups.

Following the conversion to the new Comptrollership group (CT-FIN, CT-EAV and CT-IAU) in September 2023, more information will be provided on timelines for redirecting membership dues for those employees represented by ACFO, but who are currently remitting dues to the Professional Institute of the Public Service of Canada and the Public Service Alliance of Canada.

Employees should direct any questions on this matter to [info@acfo-acaf.com](mailto:info@acfo-acaf.com).

Original signed by

Wendy Braithwaite Director, Compensation Policy, Interpretations, and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
