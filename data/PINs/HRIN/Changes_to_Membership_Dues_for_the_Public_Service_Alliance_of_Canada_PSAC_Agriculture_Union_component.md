# Changes to Membership Dues for the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) Agriculture Union component

Date: September 1, 2022

To: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre.

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective September 1, 2022.

On that date, the Agriculture Union component’s portion of the dues rate will decrease from 0.7255% to 0.6939% per member per month.

The required adjustments will be made automatically.

Employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the [Public Service Alliance of Canada](http://psacunion.ca/contact-us).

Original signed by

Angelo Gatto A/Director Compensation Policy, Interpretations and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
