# Carry-over and liquidation of vacation leave credits for certain excluded or unrepresented employees

Date: May 5, 2010

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

With the April 1, 2009 implementation of the Directive on Terms and Conditions of Employment for Certain Excluded / Unrepresented Employees, the majority of excluded or unrepresented groups and levels are now subject to the provisions of the relevant collective agreement. Consequently, the administration of vacation leave credits carry-over for excluded or unrepresented employees occupying positions at the groups and levels identified in Table 1 below, is now governed by the relevant collective agreement.

As a consequence of this change some affected employees may, on March 31, 2011, have vacation leave credits in excess of the maximum carry-over and as such, these will be liquidated as specified in the relevant collective agreement.

| Impacted Groups and Levels |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AR – 7 | EN-SUR 6 | PG – 6 |
| AS – 7, 8 | FI – 4 | PM – 6, 7 |
| AU – 5 | IS – 6 | PM-MCO-1 to 4 |
| CH – 5 | MA – 1 to 7 | PS – 5 |
| CO – 4 | MD-MSP – 2 | SE-REM – 1, 2 |
| CS -5 | MT – 8 | SG-PAT – 8 |
| DE – 3 | NU-CHN – 8 | SG-SRE – 8 |
| DS – 6 | NU-HOS – 8 | UT- 1 to 4 |
| ED-EDS – 5, 6 | PC – 5 | WP – 6, 7 |
| EN-ENG – 6 | PE – 6 |  |
