Date: December 8, 2011

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform departments that the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW) has advised us of a change in membership dues effective January 1, 2012.

The membership dues for the Electronics (EL) group will increase as follows:

| Classification | Current Rates | New Rates |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EL-1 | $ 65.32 | $ 67.32 |
| EL-2 | $ 76.07 | $ 78.07 |
| EL-3 | $ 83.04 | $ 85.04 |
| EL-4 | $ 87.95 | $ 89.95 |
| EL-5 | $ 96.13 | $ 98.13 |
| EL-6 | $104.80 | $106.80 |
| EL-7 | $113.09 | $115.09 |
| EL-8 | $120.50 | $122.50 |
| EL-9 | $128.55 | $130.55 |

This adjustment will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to IBEW.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official.

Original signed by

Kevin Marchand A/Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
