November 7, 2008

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association (FGDCA) has advised us of a change to membership dues, taking effect on January 1, 2009 as follows:

- Current Rate: $75.00
- New Rate: $50.00

This adjustment will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Please note that this new rate change applies to all chargehand and production supervisor employees located on the East Coast in the bargaining unit designator (BUD) 66319 who are currently paying FGDCA dues.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to FGDCA.

Sylvie Joseph Acting Director, Project Management, Internal Service Transformation and Pay Administration Labour Relations and Compensation Operations
