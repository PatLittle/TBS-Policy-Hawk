Date: April 30, 2013

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) notified us of a change in membership dues effective May 1, 2013.

A special levy of $2.00 per member per month has been implemented for all members in the Agriculture Union.

The component portion of dues for the Agriculture Union remains unchanged at the rate of 0.6922%.

This adjustment will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding this change to membership dues are to be directed to the Agriculture Union.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Barry Fennessy Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
