Date: May 11, 2012

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Military Colleges Faculty Association has advised us of changes in membership dues effective July 1, 2012, as follows.

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| UT-1 | $46.50 | $47.15 |
| UT-2 | $57.70 | $58.50 |
| UT-3 | $70.10 | $71.15 |
| UT-4 | $83.20 | $84.35 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the Canadian Military Colleges Faculty Association.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Michael Holt A/Senior Director Core Public Administration Compensation Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
