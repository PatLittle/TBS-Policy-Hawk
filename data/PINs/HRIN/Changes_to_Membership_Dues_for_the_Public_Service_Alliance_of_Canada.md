# Changes to Membership Dues for the Public Service Alliance of Canada

Date: January 6, 2026

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and the Public Service Pay Centre

The Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has informed us of authorized union dues rate changes effective January 1, 2026, as follows:

## Agriculture Union (AGR)

At its Triennial Convention from August 16 to 18, 2023, the Agriculture Union adopted a dues rate decrease effective January 1, 2026. The component portion will decrease from 0.7069% to 0.7067% per month.

## Union of Health and Environment Workers (UHEW)

At its Triennial Convention from August 15 to 17, 2023, the Union of Health and Environment Workers adopted a dues rate increase effective January 1, 2026. The component portion will increase and include a Component strike fund levy, with the dues changing from 0.4895% + $5.00 to 0.4907 + $5.00.

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the [Public Service Alliance of Canada](https://psacunion.ca/contact-us) or their respective [PSAC Component](https://psacunion.ca/components).
