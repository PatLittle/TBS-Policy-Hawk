# Rates of pay for unrepresented and excluded employees of certain specific occupational groups: PE, OM, AS, IS, WP

Date: October 5, 2017

To: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

I am pleased to announce the revised rates of pay as approved by the Treasury Board of Canada for unrepresented and excluded positions in the Personnel Administration (PE), Organization and Methods (OM), Administrative Services (AS), Information Services (IS) and Welfare Programmes (WP) Groups.

In addition, a $650 lump sum payment is payable to eligible employees in these unrepresented and excluded positions.

These [rates of pay](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.html) are available on the Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat Website.

The [questions and answers](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/collective-agreements/collective-bargaining/collective-bargaining-update/frequently-asked-questions-employees-core-public-administration-collective-agreement-implementation.html) regarding eligibility for the lump sum are available on the TBS Website.

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required will direct their questions to the Compensation and Labour Relations group at the following email address: [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Original signed by Zia Proulx Senior Director Core Public Administration Compensation Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
