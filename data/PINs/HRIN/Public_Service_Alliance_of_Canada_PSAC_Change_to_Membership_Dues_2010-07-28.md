July 28, 2010

To: Heads, Compensation Heads, Labour Relations

The Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of a change in membership dues effective September 1, 2010.

This change in membership dues applies to the Operational Services (SV) group, as a result of the salary increase effective August 5, 2010.

This adjustment will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact us at [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to PSAC.

Marc Thibodeau Director Compensation Operations Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
