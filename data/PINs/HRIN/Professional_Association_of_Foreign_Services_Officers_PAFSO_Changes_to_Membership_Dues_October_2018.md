# Professional Association of Foreign Services Officers (PAFSO) – Changes to Membership Dues – October 2018

Date: September 20, 2018

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this bulletin is to inform you that the Professional Association of Foreign Services Officers (PAFSO) has informed us of changes in the monthly membership dues, effective October 1, 2018.

The following chart includes the exact deduction amounts:

| Classification | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| FS-01 | $58.00 | $64.00 |
| FS-02 | $86.00 | $90.00 |
| FS-03 | $96.00 | $102.00 |
| FS-04 | $108.00 | $114.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee inquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to PAFSO.

Original signed by Terry Findlay A/Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
