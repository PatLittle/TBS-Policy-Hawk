Date: 22 October 2010

To: Heads, Compensation, Heads, Labour Relations

The Union of Environment Workers Component of Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of a change in membership dues effective January 1, 2011.

The component's portion of the dues deduction will increase from 0.5990% to 0.6264%.

Addendum

This adjustment will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact us at [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to their respective PSAC Component.

Original signed by

Marc Thibodeau A/Senior Director Compensation Management, Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
