Date: February 25, 2011

To: Heads, Compensation; Heads, Labour Relations

The Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of a change in membership dues.

PSAC has just recently adjusted their salary table for the Technical Services (TC) Group effective June 22, 2010. As a result, membership dues will be increased effective April 1, 2011.

This adjustment will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact us at [Contact Interpretations by E-mail Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to PSAC.

Marc Thibodeau A/Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
