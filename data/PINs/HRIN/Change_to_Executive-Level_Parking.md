Date: June 4, 2012

To: Heads of Human Resources, Executive Services and Compensation

The Chief Human Resources Officer has communicated to deputy heads concerning the elimination of the parking benefit for executives.

Effective July 1, 2012, this change applies to executives in the core public administration and other senior personnel who benchmark their terms and conditions of employment to those of executives.

Instructions on the elimination of the parking benefit for executives and other senior personnel to whom the change applies will be sent to applicable organizations.

Yazmine Laroche Assistant Deputy Minister Executive Policies and Talent Management Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer

Marc-Arthur Hyppolite Assistant Deputy Minister Compensation and Labour Relations Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
