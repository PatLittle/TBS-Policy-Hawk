# Canadian Federal Pilots Association - Change to Membership Dues

August 18, 2020

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

Subject: Canadian Federal Pilots Association - Change to Membership Dues

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Federal Pilots Association (CFPA) has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective September 1, 2020.

Details follow:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CAI-1 | - | $131.54 |
| CAI-2 | $117.05 | $139.86 |
| CAI-3 | $123.14 | $148.40 |
| CAI-4 | $131.05 | $156.72 |
| CAI-5 | $140.25 | $167.80 |
| ETP-1 | $150.72 | $180.40 |
| HPS-1 | $119.81 | $143.20 |
| HPS-2 | $124.56 | $148.90 |

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the Canadian Federal Pilots Association.

Original signed by

Zia Proulx Senior Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
