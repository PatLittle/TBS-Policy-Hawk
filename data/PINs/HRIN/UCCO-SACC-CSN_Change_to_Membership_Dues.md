Date: July 4, 2007

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Union of Canadian Correctional Officers has advised us of a membership dues increase for members at the CX-1 and CX-2 levels, from 1.85% to 1.95%, taking effect on July 9, 2007.

These changes will be made in the pay system automatically by PWGSC. No departmental input will be required.

Please note that this rate change applies to all employees in BUD 601 who are currently paying UCCO-SACC-CSN dues.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations Managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee inquiries regarding these union dues changes are to be directed to UCCO-SACC-SNC.

Original Signed by

Sylvie Joseph Acting Director, Project Management, Internal Service Transformation and Pay Administration Labour Relations and Compensation Operations
