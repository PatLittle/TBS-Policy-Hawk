# Personnel Administration Group (PE) - Increment Date

Date: July 10, 2009

To: Chiefs of Compensation

To ensure the ongoing uniform application of the PE increment date across the Public Service, this bulletin confirms that the PE increment date is to be administered in accordance with that applicable to the PM Group of the current PA collective agreement. (See [appendix A-2 of the Program and Administrative Services Collective Agreement](http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/pubs_pol/hrpubs/coll_agre/pa/pa08-eng.asp#app2)).

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials.

Carl Trottier Senior Director Collective Bargaining Compensation and Labour Relations
