Date: December 14, 2012

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective January 1, 2013.

There will be a dues increase from 0.9007% to 0.9668% for all employees covered by a contract with the Public Service Alliance of Canada.

As well, the following two additional changes are to be made by component:

- Agriculture Component: The component portion will decrease from 0.6951% to 0.6922%.
- Union of Environment Workers: The component portion will increase from 0.5750% to 0.5880%.

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding these changes to membership dues are to be directed to the employee's respective Public Service Alliance of Canada component.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Kevin Marchand Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
