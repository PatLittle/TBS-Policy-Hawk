# Annual Update to the Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive - Appendices K-1 and K-2

August 1, 2018

To: Functional Heads of Administration, Human Resources and Financial Service

CC: Chairs and Executive Directors of Northern Federal Regional Councils, Departmental Designated Coordinators for the IPGHD, Committee members of the NJC IPGHD (for information)

Please distribute this notice to persons at your headquarters and regional offices who are involved in the administration of the allowances.

The Shelter Cost Differential rates applicable to employees who are in private accommodation in Inuvik, NT, Iqaluit, NU, Norman Wells, NT, Whitehorse, YT and Yellowknife, NT for the period of August 1, 2017 to July 31, 2018 are now available in [Appendix K-1](https://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/d4/v237/s634/en#s634-tc-tm) of the Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive.

The Shelter Cost Differential rates applicable to employees who are in Government housing in Inuvik, NT, Iqaluit, NU, and Yellowknife, NT for the period of August 1, 2017 to July 31, 2018 are now available in [Appendix K-2](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/d4/v237/s635/en) of the Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive.

Any questions should be directed to the appropriate [IPGHD Designated Departmental Coordinators](http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/ap/list-liste/ddhc-cmdl-eng.asp) who, as required, can contact the appropriate officers at the Treasury Board Secretariat.

Claudia Zovatto Senior Director, Union Engagement & NJC Support Compensation and Labour Relations Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
