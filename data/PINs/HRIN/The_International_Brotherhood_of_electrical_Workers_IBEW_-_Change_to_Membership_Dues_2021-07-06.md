# The International Brotherhood of electrical Workers (IBEW) - Change to Membership Dues

Date: June 29, 2021 To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi Subject: The International Brotherhood of electrical Workers (IBEW) - Change to Membership Dues

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the International Brotherhood of electrical Workers (IBEW) has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective September 1, 2021.

Details follow:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EL-1 | $83.64 | $84.92 |
| EL-2 | $96.70 | $98.24 |
| EL-3 | $105.18 | $106.86 |
| EL-4 | $111.17 | $113.00 |
| EL-5 | $121.11 | $123.14 |
| EL-6 | $131.66 | $133.90 |
| EL-7 | $141.75 | $144.20 |
| EL-8 | $148.82 | $151.42 |
| EL-9 | $160.54 | $163.38 |

These adjustments will be made automatically, no further action is required.

Employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the [IBEW](mailto:office@ibew2228.ca).

Original signed by

Angelo Gatto A/Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Employment Conditions and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
