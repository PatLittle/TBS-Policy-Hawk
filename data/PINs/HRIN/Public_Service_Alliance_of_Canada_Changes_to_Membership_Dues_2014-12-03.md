Date: December 3, 2014

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective January 1, 2015.

There will be changes to membership dues for each of the following components :

- Agriculture Union: The component portion will increase from 0.6921% to 0.7023%.
- Union of Environment Workers: The component portion will decrease from 0.6117% to 0.5610%.
- Union of Veterans Affairs Employees: The component portion will increase from 0.5781% to 0.5960%.
- National Health Union: The component portion will remain at 0.5000%. A flat rate of $0.60 per member per month will be added.
- National Resources Union: The component portion will increase from 0.7700% to 0.8150%.
- Union of Solicitor General Employees: The component portion will increase from 0.6873% to 0.7048%.
- Union of National Employees: The component portion will increase from 0.5433% to 0.6000%.

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the Public Service Alliance of Canada.

Original signed by

Virginie Emiel-Wildhaber A/Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
