Date: February 21, 2014

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The purpose of this bulletin is to inform you that the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers has advised us of changes in monthly membership dues effective April 1, 2014.

The new membership rates are listed below.

| Classification | New rates |
| --- | --- |
| EL-1 | $ 72.94 |
| EL-2 | $ 84.44 |
| EL-3 | $ 91.88 |
| EL-4 | $ 97.14 |
| EL-5 | $105.88 |
| EL-6 | $115.15 |
| EL-7 | $124.02 |
| EL-8 | $131.94 |
| EL-9 | $140.54 |

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Original signed by:

Kevin Marchand Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
