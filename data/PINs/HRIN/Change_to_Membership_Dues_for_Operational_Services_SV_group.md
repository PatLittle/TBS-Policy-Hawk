# Change to Membership Dues for Operational Services (SV) group

Date: August 10, 2012

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour; Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada has advised us of a change in membership dues effective September 1, 2012.

This change in membership dues applies to the Operational Services (SV) group, as a result of salary increases effective August 5, 2012 reflected in the collective agreement signed on April 6, 2011.

This adjustment will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the Public Service Alliance of Canada.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Michael Holt A/Senior Director Core Public Administration Compensation Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
