Date: May 10, 2013

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada has advised us of changes in membership dues effective July 1, 2013.

These changes in membership dues apply to the following groups:

- Program and Administrative Services (PA), as a result of salary increases scheduled for June 21, 2013; and
- Education and Library Science (EB), as a result of salary increases scheduled for July 1, 2013.

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding these changes to membership dues are to be directed to the Public Service Alliance of Canada.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Barry Fennessy Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
