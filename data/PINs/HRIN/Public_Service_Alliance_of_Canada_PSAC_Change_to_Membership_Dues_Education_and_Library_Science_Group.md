Date: May 29, 2009

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of an upcoming change to membership dues, resulting from the signing of the collective agreement.

This change in membership dues applies to the Education and Library Science Group and is effective July 1, 2009.

This adjustment will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to their respective PSAC Component.

Original signed by

Sylvie Joseph

Director, Project Management, Internal Service Transformation and Pay Administration Labour Relations and Compensation Operations
