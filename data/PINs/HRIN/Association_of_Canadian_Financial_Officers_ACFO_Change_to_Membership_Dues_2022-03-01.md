# Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO) – Change to Membership Dues

Date: March 1, 2022

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform departments that the Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO) has advised us of changes in the monthly membership dues, for Financial Management (FI) effective April 1, 2022.

The monthly membership dues will increase from $62.42 to $65.42.

Any employee inquiries, regarding this membership dues change, are to be directed to ACFO, [info@acfo-acaf.com](mailto:info@acfo-acaf.com).

Original signed by

Angelo Gatto A/Director Compensation Policy, Interpretations and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
