Date: November 10, 2009

To: Heads of Compensation, Heads of Labour Relations

The Professional Association of Foreign Service Officers (PAFSO) has advised us of a change to membership dues, effective January 1, 2010.

The membership dues deductions will be reduced as indicated in the table below.

| Classification | Current Rates | New Rates |
| --- | --- | --- |
| FS-01 | $56.00 | $55.00 |
| FS-02 | $74.00 | $73.00 |
| FS-03 | $94.00 | $91.00 |
| FS-04 | $101.00 | $99.00 |

This adjustment will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Compensation Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to PAFSO.

Marc Thibodeau Director CPA Compensation Operations Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
