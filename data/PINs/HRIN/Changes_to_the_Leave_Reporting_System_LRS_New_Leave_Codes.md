# Changes to the Leave Reporting System (LRS) - New Leave Codes

Date: February 11, 2011

To: Heads of Compensation; Heads of Labour Relations

This bulletin provides information regarding new leave codes, created in the Leave Reporting System (LRS) as part of leave block series 400 - Family Related Leave Responsibilities (FRR).

On December 21, 2010, you were advised of changes in leave for FRR as a result of re-opening the Program and Administrative Services (PA), Education and Library Science (EB) and Operational Services (SV) groups’ collective agreements. As a result, the following three new LRS codes have been created:

- Code 470 – School functions
- Code 471 – Unforeseeable school/daycare closure
- Code 472 – Appointment with Professional

It is requested that departments ensure their leave systems are amended.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who may send their enquiries to the following e-mail address: [Contact Interpretations by E-mail Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Marc Thibodeau A/Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
