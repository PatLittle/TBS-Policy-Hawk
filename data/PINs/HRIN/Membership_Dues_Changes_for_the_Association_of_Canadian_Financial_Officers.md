# Membership Dues Changes for the Association of Canadian Financial Officers

August 12, 2008

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO) has advised us of a membership dues rate change, taking effect on July 1, 2008.

The ACFO membership dues have increased from $25.00 to $35.00.

These adjustments were made automatically to the pay system. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations Managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to ACFO.

Sylvie Joseph Acting Director, Project Management, Internal Service Transformation and Pay Administration Labour Relations and Compensation Operation
