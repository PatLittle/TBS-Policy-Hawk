Date: June 27, 2013

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Military Colleges Faculty Association (CMCFA) has advised us of changes in membership dues effective September 1, 2013, as follows:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| UT–1 | $47.15 | $54.90 |
| UT–2 | $58.50 | $68.15 |
| UT–3 | $71.15 | $82.85 |
| UT–4 | $84.35 | $98.25 |

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding these changes to membership dues are to be directed to the Canadian Military Colleges Faculty Association.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Original signed by

Barry Fennessy Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
