# Rates of Pay for Excluded Employees of Certain Specific Occupational Groups 2014-2017

**Date**: July 14, 2017

**To**: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

I am pleased to announce the revised rates of pay for excluded employees, as approved by Treasury Board of Canada, for the following groups: Audit, Commerce and Purchasing (AV), Financial Management (FI), Research (RE), Radio Operations (RO), Applied Science and Patent examination (SP) and Translation (TR).

## Rates of Pay

The rates of pay for those excluded positions will be available shortly on the TBS Web site.

The [attached appendices](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.html) provide the rates of pay for those excluded positions.

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required will direct their questions to the Compensation and Labour Relations group at the following email address: [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Original signed by Zia Proulx Senior Director Core Public Administration Compensation Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
