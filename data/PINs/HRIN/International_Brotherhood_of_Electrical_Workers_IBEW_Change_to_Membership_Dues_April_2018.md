# International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW) – Change to Membership Dues – April 2018

**Date:**: April 13, 2018

**To:**: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this bulletin is to inform you that the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW) has informed us of changes in the monthly membership dues, effective April 1, 2018.

The following chart includes the exact deduction amounts:

| Classification | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EL-1 | $72.94 | $78.97 |
| EL-2 | $84.44 | $91.29 |
| EL-3 | $91.88 | $99.27 |
| EL-4 | $97.14 | $104.91 |
| EL-5 | $105.88 | $114.28 |
| EL-6 | $115.15 | $124.22 |
| EL-7 | $124.02 | $133.72 |
| EL-8 | $131.93 | $142.21 |
| EL-9 | $140.54 | $151.43 |

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee inquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to IBEW.

Original signed by Terry Findlay a/Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
