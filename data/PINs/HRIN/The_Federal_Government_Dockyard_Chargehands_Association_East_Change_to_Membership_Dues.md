# The Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association (East) – Change to Membership Dues

Date: June 2, 2023

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and the Public Service Pay Centre

The Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association (FGDCA - EAST) has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective July 1, 2023.

|  | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly | 5.00$ | 50.00$ |
| Bi-Weekly | 2.50$ | 25.00$ |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Employees should direct any questions on this matter to [fgdcapresident@gmail.com](mailto:fgdcapresident@gmail.com).

Original signed by

Wendy Braithwaite Director, Compensation Policy, Interpretations, and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
