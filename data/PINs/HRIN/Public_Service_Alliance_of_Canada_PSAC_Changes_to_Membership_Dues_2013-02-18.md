Date: February 18, 2013

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective April 1, 2013.

These changes in membership dues apply to the Firefighters (FR) and the Ships’ Crews (SC) groups, as a result of salary increases effective April 1, 2013.

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding these changes to membership dues are to be directed to the employee’s respective Public Service Alliance of Canada component.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation Official.

Kevin Marchand A/Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
