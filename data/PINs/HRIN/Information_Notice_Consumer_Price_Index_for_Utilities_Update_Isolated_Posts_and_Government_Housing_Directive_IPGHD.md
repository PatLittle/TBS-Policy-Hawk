# Information Notice - Consumer Price Index for Utilities Update - Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive (IPGHD)

Date: April 1st, 2017

To: Functional Heads of Administration, Human Resources and Financial Service, Designated Departmental Contacts of the IPGHD

The purpose of this notice is to provide departments and agencies with the revised Consumer Price Index (CPI) for utilities, in order to make the appropriate rent adjustments for public servants living in government housing. Departments are reminded to distribute this notice to employees at your headquarters and regional offices who are involved in the administration of the isolated posts allowances.

## January 2017 CPI Index:

- As mentioned in subsection 6.14.2 of the IPGHD, the CPI, which is 159.0 for January 2017, is to be used in the calculation of utilities charges.
- Note: The monthly CPI Movement can be found online at the following web link: [Table 4-2 The Consumer Price Index – Shelter](http://www.statcan.gc.ca/pub/62-001-x/2017001/t034-eng.htm) – Water, Fuel and Electricity row for January 2017

Please ensure that this notice is provided to those responsible for calculating the government housing rental charge increases of 2017.

As per subsection 6.12.2 of the IPGHD, employees must receive a three month notification (by April 30, 2017) of the rental charge increases which come into effect on August 1, 2017.

Employees should direct any questions on this subject to the appropriate corporate official for their organization. [View the list of Designated Contacts](http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/pubs_pol/hrpubs/ipgh-dpill/ddhc-cmdl-eng.asp).

Claudia Zovatto Senior Director, Compensation and Labour Relations Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
