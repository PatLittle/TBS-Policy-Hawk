Date: November 12, 2009

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Canadian Auto Workers (CAW) Local 2182 has advised us of changes to membership dues effective January 1, 2010 and May 1, 2010.

Please find attached two tables indicating the pay levels and the appropriate rates, for each of these changes.

These adjustments will have to be made manually within departments. Public Works and Government Services Canada (PWGSC) will be sending out a broadcast message with details on how to process these changes which need to be completed by January 5, 2010.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Compensation Administration Section.

Any employee inquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to CAW Local 2182.

Marc Thibodeau Director CPA Compensation Operations Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
