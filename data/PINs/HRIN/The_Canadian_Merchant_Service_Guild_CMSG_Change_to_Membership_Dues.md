Date: August 19, 2011

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform departments that the Canadian Merchant Service Guild (CMSG) has advised us of a change in membership dues effective December 1, 2011.

The membership dues for the Ships' Officers (SO) group will increase as follows:

| Current rate | New rate |
| --- | --- |
| $94.25 | $96.00 |

The membership dues rate for the Canadian Coast Guard Officer Cadets remains unchanged at $11.00.

This adjustment will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to CMSG.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official.

Original signed by

Marc Thibodeau Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
