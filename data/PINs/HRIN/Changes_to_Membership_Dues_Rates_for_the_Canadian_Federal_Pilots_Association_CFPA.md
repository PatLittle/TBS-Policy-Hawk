# Changes to Membership Dues Rates for the Canadian Federal Pilots Association (CFPA)

Date: March 2, 2011

To: Heads, Compensation; Heads, Labour Relations

The Canadian Federal Pilots Association (CFPA) has advised us of upcoming changes to membership dues rates effective May 1, 2011.

Details follow:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CAI-2 | $91.99 | $110.38 |
| CAI-3 | $97.51 | $117.01 |
| CAI-4 | $102.90 | $123.48 |
| CAI-5 | $110.07 | $132.08 |
| ETP-1 | $118.23 | $141.87 |
| HPS-1 | $94.14 | $112.97 |
| HPS-2 | $97.84 | $117.40 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No department input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, may contact us at [Contact Interpretations by E-mail Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues rate changes are to be directed to CFPA.

Marc Thibodeau A/Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
