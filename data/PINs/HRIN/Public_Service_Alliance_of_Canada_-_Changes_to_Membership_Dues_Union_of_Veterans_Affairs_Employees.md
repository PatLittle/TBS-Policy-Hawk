# Public Service Alliance of Canada - Changes to Membership Dues (Union of Veterans' Affairs Employees)

**Date:**: May 8, 2018

**To:**: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective June 1, 2018.

The special levy will increase from $3.70 per member per month to $5.80 per member per month which will be implemented for all members of the Union of Veterans' Affairs Employees.

The component portion of dues for the Union of Veterans' Affairs Employees remains unchanged at the rate of 0.5960%.

As this increase will require changes to the Phoenix system, this notice represents an advance notice of the upcoming change.

Once this information is updated in the pay system, members will see an adjustment in their dues.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to their respective Component, the Union of Veterans' Affairs Employees, at (613) 560-5460 or at [http://www.uvae-seac.ca](http://www.uvae-seac.ca).

Original signed by Terry Findlay a/Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
