# Rates of Pay for Unrepresented Employees of the PM-MCO Sub-group

**Date:**: January 17, 2018

**To**: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

I am pleased to announce that the Treasury Board has approved new rates of pay for unrepresented employees in the Program Administration Mediation Conciliation (PM-MCO) Sub-Group.

The [attached appendix](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.html#Toc476385576) provides the rates of pay for the unrepresented positions. In addition, a $650 lump sum payment is payable to eligible employees in these unrepresented positions.

The new rates of pay will be available on the TBS Website in the near future.

The [questions and answers](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/collective-agreements/collective-bargaining/collective-bargaining-update/frequently-asked-questions-employees-core-public-administration-collective-agreement-implementation.html) regarding eligibility for the lump sum are available on the TBS Website.

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required will direct their questions to the Compensation and Labour Relations group at the following email address: [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca)

Original signed by

Zia Proulx Senior Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
