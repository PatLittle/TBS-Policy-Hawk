# Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association – Change to Membership Dues

Date: February 12, 2021

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

Subject: Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association – Change to Membership Dues

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association (FGDCA) has changed the rate of its monthly membership dues for Ships Repair (All Chargehands and Production Supervisor Employees located on the East Coast) (SR(C)) effective December 1, 2020. The change took place in the pay system, February 3, 2021 and over contributions will be reimbursed soon after the effective date.

Membership dues will decrease as follows:

| Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- |
| $40.00 | $5.00 |

This adjustment will be made automatically, no further action is required.

Employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association, [fgdca@hushmail.com](mailto:fgdca@hushmail.com).

Original signed by

Angelo Gatto Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Employment Conditions Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
