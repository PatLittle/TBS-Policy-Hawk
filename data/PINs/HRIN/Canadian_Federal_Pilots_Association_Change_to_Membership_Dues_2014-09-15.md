Date: September 15, 2014

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Federal Pilots Association has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective September 1, 2014.

Details follow :

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CAI-2 | $110.38 | $117.05 |
| CAI-3 | $117.01 | $123.14 |
| CAI-4 | $123.48 | $131.05 |
| CAI-5 | $132.08 | $140.25 |
| ETP-1 | $141.87 | $150.72 |
| HPS-1 | $112.97 | $119.81 |
| HPS-2 | $117.40 | $124.56 |

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the Canadian Federal Pilots Association.

Original signed by

Virginie Emiel-Wildhaber A/Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
