May 1, 2008

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The FGDCA has advised us of membership dues rate changes, taking effect on June 1, 2008 as follows:

| Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- |
| $100.00 | $75.00 |

These adjustments will be made in the pay system automatically by Public Works and Government Services Canada (PWGSC). No departmental input will be required.

Please note that this new rate is applicable to all chargehand and production supervisor employees located on the East Coast in the bargaining unit designator (BUD) 66319 who are currently paying FGDCA dues.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations Managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee inquiries regarding these union dues changes are to be directed to FGDCA.

Sylvie Joseph Acting Director, Project Management, Internal Service Transformation and Pay Administration Labour Relations and Compensation Operations
