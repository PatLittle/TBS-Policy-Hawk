Date: May 29, 2009

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

Subject: Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) - Change to Membership Dues

The Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of upcoming changes to membership dues, resulting from the signing of the collective agreement.

These changes in membership dues apply to the Operational Services Group and are effective July 1, 2009 and September 1, 2009.

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to their respective PSAC Component.

Original signed by

Sylvie Joseph

Director, Project Management, Internal Service Transformation and Pay Administration Labour Relations and Compensation Operations
