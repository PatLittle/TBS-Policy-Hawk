# Payment in Arrears Update

Date: February 24, 2014

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

Further to the recent Public Works and Government Services Canada updates on Pay Modernization, the purpose of this information bulletin is to advise you that the implementation date to payment in arrears has been postponed four (4) weeks.

Please visit the Public Works and Government Services Canada website on [Transformation of Pay Administration Initiative](http://www.tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca/remuneration-compensation/projets-projects/tpai-itap/index-eng.html) for further details.

Original signed by:

Kevin Marchand Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
