# Canadian Federal Pilots Association - Change to Membership Dues

Date: February 10, 2021

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Federal Pilots Association (CFPA) has changed the rate of its monthly membership dues, effective February 1, 2021.

Details follow:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CAI-1 | $65.77 | $66.76 |
| CAI-2 | $69.93 | $70.98 |
| CAI-3 | $74.20 | $75.31 |
| CAI-4 | $78.36 | $79.54 |
| CAI-5 | $83.90 | $85.16 |
| ETP-1 | $90.20 | $91.55 |
| HPS-1 | $71.60 | $72.67 |
| HPS-2 | $74.45 | $75.57 |

These adjustments will be made automatically, no further action is required.

Employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the Canadian Federal Pilots Association, [cfpa@cfpa-apfc.ca](mailto:cfpa@cfpa-apfc.ca).

Original signed by Angelo Gatto A/Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Employment Conditions and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
