Date: August 9, 2013

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Association of Professional Employees (CAPE) has advised us of a change in membership dues effective September 1, 2013.

Membership dues will increase as follows:

| Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- |
| $35.00 | $43.00 |

This adjustment will be made automatically.

Any employee enquiries regarding this change to membership dues are to be directed to the Canadian Association of Professional Employees.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation official.

Kevin Marchand Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officers
