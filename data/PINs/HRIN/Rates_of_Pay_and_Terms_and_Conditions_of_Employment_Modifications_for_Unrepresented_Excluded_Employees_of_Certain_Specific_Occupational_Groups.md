# Rates of Pay and Terms and Conditions of Employment Modifications for Unrepresented /Excluded Employees of Certain Specific Occupational Groups

Date: July 9, 2015

To: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

I am pleased to announce the revised rates of pay as well as modifications to the Terms and Conditions of Employment for certain unrepresented/excluded employees, as approved by the Treasury Board of Canada, for the following groups: Defence Scientific Service Group (levels 7A, 7B and 8) and the Law Management Group.

## Rates of Pay

The rates of pay applicable to these unrepresented/excluded employee groups can be found online at: [Rates of Pay for Certain Excluded and Unrepresented Employees](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.html).

The attached Appendices [A](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/information-notice/rates-pay-terms-conditions-employment-modifications-unrepresented-excluded-employees-certain-specific-occupational-groups.html#appA) and [B](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/information-notice/rates-pay-terms-conditions-employment-modifications-unrepresented-excluded-employees-certain-specific-occupational-groups.html#appB) report the rates of pay for those unrepresented or excluded positions.

## Performance Pay

As indicated in correspondence sent out from the Chief Human Resources Officer Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat, changes made to the administration of the corporate commitment will be implemented for the 2015-16 performance cycle. Please refer to their correspondence sent on July 6, 2015 for further details as these changes will also apply to the Defence Scientific Service Group (levels 7A, 7B and 8) and the Law Management Group.

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required will direct their questions to the Compensation and Labour Relations group at the following email address: [Contact Interpretations by E-mail : Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Kevin Marchand Senior Director Core Public Administration Compensation Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer

### Pay Notes

1. A DS-7A, DS-7B or DS-08 employee not yet at the salary range maximum shall have his or her rate of pay increased:
1. On April 1, 2014 , to a rate within the "A" performance pay range which is zero point five percent (0.5%) higher than his or her former rate of pay;
2. On April 1, 2015 , to a rate within the "B" performance pay range which is zero point five percent (0.5%) higher than his or her former rate of pay.

2. Round to nearest $100.
3. Approval Date: June 18, 2015.
