# Change to Membership Dues for the Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO)

Date: January 28, 2026

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO) has changed the rate of its monthly membership dues, effective April 1, 2026.

Membership dues are increasing for all members of the Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO) as follows:

| Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- |
| $73.18 | $74.94 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to [info@acfo-acaf.com](mailto:info@acfo-acaf.com).

Original signed by

Wendy Bennen Director, Compensation Policy, Interpretations, and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
