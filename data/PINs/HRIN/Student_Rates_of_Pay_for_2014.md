# Student Rates of Pay for 2014

Date: February 4, 2014

To: Heads of Human Resources; Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The Secretary of the Treasury Board has approved new rates of pay effective January 1, 2014 for all students hired under Treasury Board Student Employment Programs.

The new rates for 2014 reflect a one decimal five percent (1.5%) increase on the 2013 rates.

The new [rates of pay](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/student-rates-pay-effective-january-1-2014.html) will be available shortly on the TBS Web site.

Kevin Marchand Director Pay Administration and Policy Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
