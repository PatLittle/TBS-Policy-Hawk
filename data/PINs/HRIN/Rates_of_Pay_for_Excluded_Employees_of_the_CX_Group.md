# Rates of Pay for Excluded Employees of the CX Group

Date: May 4, 2022

To: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this bulletin is to announce the revised rates of pay as approved by the Treasury Board of Canada for excluded positions in the Correctional Services (CX) Supervisory group.

The attached appendix provides the rates of pay for the excluded positions.

The new [rates of pay](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.html#Toc476385500) are available on the TBS Website.

All questions should be directed to your Designated Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Designated Corporate Compensation Official who, if required, will direct their questions to the Compensation Policy, Interpretations and Pay Administration team, at the following email address: [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Original signed by

Angelo Gatto A/Director Compensation Policy, Interpretations and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
