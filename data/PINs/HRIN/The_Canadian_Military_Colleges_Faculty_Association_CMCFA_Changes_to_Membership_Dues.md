Date: June 30, 2009

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Canadian Military Colleges Faculty Association (CMCFA) has advised us of changes to membership dues for the University Teaching (UT) Group, effective July 1, 2009 as follows:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| UT-1 | $44.40 | $45.10 |
| UT-2 | $54.30 | $55.10 |
| UT-3 | $65.60 | $66.50 |
| UT-4 | $77.90 | $79.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No department input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations Managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to CMCFA.

Original signed by

Carl Trottier

Senior Director Collective Bargaining Labour Relations and Compensation Operations
