Date: March 16th, 2012

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform departments that the Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association (FGDCA) has advised us of a change in membership dues effective May 1, 2012.

The membership dues for all chargehand and production supervisor employees located on the East Coast will increase as follows.

| Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- |
| $50.00 | $75.00 |

This adjustment will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Any employee inquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to FGDCA.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official.

Original signed by

Michael Holt A/Senior Director Core Public Administration Compensation Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
