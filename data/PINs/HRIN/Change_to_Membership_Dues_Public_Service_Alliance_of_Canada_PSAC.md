Date: November 12, 2009

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of a change to membership dues.

The dues deductions will increase from 0.8963% plus $1.00 per month to 0.9007% plus $1.00 per month, effective January 1, 2010.

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Compensation Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to PSAC.

Marc Thibodeau Director CPA Compensation Operations Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
