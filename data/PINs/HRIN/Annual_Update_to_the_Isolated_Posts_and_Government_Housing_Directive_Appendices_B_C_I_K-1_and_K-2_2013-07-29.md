Date: July 29, 2013

To: Functional Heads of Administration, Human Resources and Financial Service

CC: Chairs and Executive Directors of Northern Federal Regional Councils, Departmental Designated Coordinators for the Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive (IPGHD), Committee members of the National Joint Council IPGHD (for information)

Please distribute this notice and the amended appendices of the National Joint Council Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive to persons at your headquarters and regional offices who are involved in the administration of the allowances.

The annual update of the IPGHD rates has recently been approved by the National Joint Council IPGHD committee in accordance with the approved methodologies. Details regarding the methodology can be found in the "[Guide to National Joint Council Rates and Allowances](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/doc.php?did=117%20&lang=en)". The changes in Appendices B, C, I, K1 and K2 are effective August 1, 2013 and will be administered in accordance with section 2.9.2 of the IPGHD.

The National Joint Council IPGHD has been amended to reflect these changes:

1. The [Environment Allowance rates](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=127&lang=eng), Appendix B, have been increased, effective August 1, 2013.
2. The [Living Cost Differential rates](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=128&lang=eng), Appendix C, have been adjusted effective August 1, 2013.
3. The Shelter Cost Differential rates, Appendix [K-1](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=136&lang=eng) and [K-2](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=137&lang=eng), have been adjusted effective August 1, 2013.
4. The meal and rations rates, [Appendix I](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=134&lang=eng), have been adjusted effective August 1, 2013.

Any questions from employees and regional staff should be directed to the appropriate [Designated Departmental IPGHD Coordinators](http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/psm-fpfm/pay-remuneration/other-autres/ddhc-cmdl-eng.asp) who, as required, can contact the appropriate officers at the Treasury Board Secretariat.

Peter Field Acting Senior Director, Union Engagement & NJC Support Compensation and Labour Relations Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
