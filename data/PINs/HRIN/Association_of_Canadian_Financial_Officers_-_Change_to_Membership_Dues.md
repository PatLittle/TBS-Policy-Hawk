# Association of Canadian Financial Officers – Change to Membership Dues

July 24, 2020

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

Subject: Association of Canadian Financial Officers – Change to Membership Dues

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO) has changed the rate of its monthly membership dues, effective April 1, 2020.

Membership dues are increasing for all members of the Financial Management (FI) group as follows:

| Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- |
| $50.00 | $62.00 |

This adjustment will be made automatically.

Employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the Association of Canadian Financial Officers.

Original signed by

Zia Proulx Senior Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
