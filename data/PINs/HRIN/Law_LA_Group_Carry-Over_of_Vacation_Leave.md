# Law (LA) Group - Carry-Over of Vacation Leave

Date: April 15, 2011

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The purpose of this bulletin is to advise departments of an error in the Law (LA) collective agreement related to the maximum quantum of vacation leave carry-over for lawyers.

Clauses 17.08 a) and 17.08 b) in the agreement expiring May 9, 2011 currently stipulate the maximum available carry-over to be one hundred and eighty-seven decimal five (187.5) hours. The maximum available carry-over for the LA group clauses 17.08 a) and 17.08 b) should read two hundred and sixty-two decimal five (262.5) hours. Please advise your managers accordingly.

The electronic version of the collective agreement posted on the TBS website will be updated.

All questions should be directed to the appropriate corporate officials.

Departmental Corporate Labour Relations and Corporate Compensation Officials are advised that all enquiries should be sent directly to the following email address: [Contact Interpretations by E-mail Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).
