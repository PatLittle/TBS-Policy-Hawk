# Changes to the Union Dues Structure for the Communications, Energy and Paperworkers Union of Canada (CEP) - Local 588G

Date: August 5, 2009

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

This bulletin is to advise you of changes to be made to the membership dues structure for the Communications, Energy and Paperworkers Union of Canada (CEP) – Local 588G. Currently, CEP uses an hourly range structure which will be changed to an hourly rate specific structure effective October 1st, 2009.

The new membership rates are contained in the enclosed document. Although the new rates included in the chart go back to 2007, CEP does not intend to recover arrears prior to October 2009, therefore the change of rates is October 1st, 2009 (as highlighted in the enclosure).

Please note that this new rate structure applies to employees in the Bindery Sub-Group of the Non-Supervisory Printing Services Group who are currently paying CEP membership dues and are covered by a collective agreement between the Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat and CEP.

Due to the large number of different rates, as well as the very low population of members (under 25), Public Works and Government Services of Canada (PWGSC) is not able to upload all these rates into the Regional Pay System (RPS). Consequently, no bulk change will be scheduled and the updates to the rates will have to be done manually by departments.

PWGSC will be issuing a broadcast message to advise compensation advisors.

Please note that every time there is a change in the hourly rate of pay, the compensation advisor will be responsible for manually adjusting the membership dues rates according to the chart provided.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to CEP.

Carl Trottier A/Executive Director Strategic Compensation Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
