# Canadian Military Colleges Faculty Association (CMCFA) - Change to Membership Dues

Date: May 31, 2021 To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi Subject: Canadian Military Colleges Faculty Association (CMCFA) - Change to Membership Dues

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Military Colleges Faculty Association (CMCFA) has advised us of changes in monthly membership dues effective June 1, 2021.

Details follow:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| UT-1 | $59.00 | $64.00 |
| UT-2 | $72.90 | $79.00 |
| UT-3 | $89.36 | $96.00 |
| UT-4 | $106.00 | $114.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically, no further action is required.

Employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the [CMCFA](mailto:execasst@cmcfa-apcmc.ca).

Original signed by

Angelo Gatto A/Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Employment Conditions and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
