# The Professional Institute of the Public Service of Canada (PIPSC) – Increase to Membership Dues

Date: December 20, 2024

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre.

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Professional Institute of the Public Service of Canada (PIPSC) has changed the rate of its monthly membership dues, effective January 1, 2025.

Membership dues are increasing for all PIPSC members as follows:

| Current rate | New rate |
| --- | --- |
| $72.56 | $90.06 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the [Professional Institute of the Public Service of Canada (PIPSC)](https://pipsc.ca/) or by email to [membership@pipsc.ca](mailto:membership@pipsc.ca).

Original signed by

Wendy Bennen Director, Compensation Policy and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
