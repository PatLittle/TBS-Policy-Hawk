Date: July 21, 2011

To: Directors/Chiefs, of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform departments that the Canadian Military Colleges Faculty Association (CMCFA) has advised us of changes to membership dues effective September 1, 2011, as follows:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| UT-1 | $45.10 | $46.50 |
| UT-2 | $55.10 | $57.70 |
| UT-3 | $66.50 | $70.10 |
| UT-4 | $79.00 | $83.20 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to CMCFA.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required, will direct their questions to the following e-mail address: [Contact Interpretations by E-mail Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Original signed by

Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
