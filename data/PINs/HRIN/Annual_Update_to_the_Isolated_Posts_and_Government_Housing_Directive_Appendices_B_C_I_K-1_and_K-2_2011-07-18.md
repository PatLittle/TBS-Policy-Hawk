Date: July 18, 2011

To: Functional Heads of Administration, Human Resources and Financial Service

CC: Chairs and Executive Directors of Northern Federal Regional Councils, Departmental Designated Coordinators for the IPGHD, Committee members of the NJC IPGHD (for information)

Please distribute this notice and the amended appendices of the National Joint Council (NJC) Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive (IPGHD) as quickly as possible to persons at your headquarters and regional offices who are involved in the administration of the allowances.

The purpose of this notice is to provide you with the approved updates to the NJC IPGHD. The changes in Appendices B, C, I, K1 and K2 will be effective August 1, 2011.

These changes were arrived at following consultations within the NJC IPGH committee and are in accordance with the approved methodologies. The NJC IPGHD has been amended to reflect these changes:

1. The [Environment Allowance rates](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=127&lang=eng), Appendix B, have been increased, effective August 1, 2011.
2. The [Living Cost Differential rates](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=128&lang=eng), Appendix C, have been adjusted effective August 1, 2011.
3. The Shelter Cost Differential rates, Appendix [K-1](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=136&lang=eng) and [K-2](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=137&lang=eng), have been adjusted effective August 1, 2011.

The meal and rations rates, [Appendix I](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=134&lang=eng), have been adjusted effective August 1, 2011. Details regarding the methodology can be found in the "[Guide to National Joint Council Rates and Allowances](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/doc.php?did=117&lang=en)".

No pay action should be taken to implement these revisions until further instructions are received from the applicable pay office of Public Works and Government Services Canada. Any retroactive payments resulting from the above changes are to be administered in the same manner as retroactive increases to employees' pay.

Any questions from employees and regional staff should be directed to the appropriate [Designated Departmental IPGHD Coordinators](http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/psm-fpfm/pay-remuneration/other-autres/ddhc-cmdl-eng.asp) who, as required, can contact the appropriate officers at the Treasury Board Secretariat.

Claudia Zovatto Senior Director, Union Engagement & NJC Support Compensation and Labour Relations Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
