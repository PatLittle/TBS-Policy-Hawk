22 December, 2008

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Union of Solicitor General Employees Component of Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has advised us of a change to membership dues, taking effect on January 1, 2009, as follows:

- Current Rate: 0.631533%
- New Rate: 0.6845%

This adjustment will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Please note that this new rate change applies to Public Safety Canada employees who are currently paying Union of Solicitor General Employees Component membership dues and are covered by a collective agreement between the Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat and the PSAC.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to their respective PSAC Component.

Sylvie Joseph Director, Project Management, Internal Service Transformation and Pay Administration Labour Relations and Compensation Operation
