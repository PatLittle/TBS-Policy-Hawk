Date: September 10, 2009

To: Heads, Compensation and Labour Relations

The Canadian Federal Pilots Association (CFPA) has advised us of upcoming changes to membership dues rates effective January 1, 2010.

Details follow:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| CAI-2 | $87.96 | $91.99 |
| CAI-3 | $93.20 | $97.51 |
| CAI-4 | $98.31 | $102.90 |
| CAI-5 | $105.11 | $110.07 |
| ETP-1 | $112.86 | $118.23 |
| HPS-1 | $93.20 | $94.14 |
| HPS-2 | $93.20 | $97.84 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No department input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact the Pay Administration Section.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues rate changes are to be directed to CFPA.

Carl Trottier Senior Director CPA Compensation Operations Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
