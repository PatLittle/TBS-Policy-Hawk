# Public Service Alliance of Canada - Changes to Membership Dues – January 2019

Date: December 12, 2018

To: Heads of Human Resources Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues, effective January 1, 2019.

The current rate of 0.974% + $1.00 per member per month will change to 0.9557% + $1.00 per member per month.

The PSAC recognizes that employees will have questions specific to their adjustments. Please direct your employees to the following link of the [PSAC website](http://psacunion.ca/uniondues).

Original signed by Terry Findlay A/Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
