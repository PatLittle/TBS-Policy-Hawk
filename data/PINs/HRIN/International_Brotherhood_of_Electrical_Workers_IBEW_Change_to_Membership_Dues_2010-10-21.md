Date: October 21, 2010

To: Heads, Compensation, Heads, Labour Relations

The International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW) has advised us of a change to membership dues effective January 1, 2011.

The following chart includes the exact deduction amounts:

| Classification | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EL-1 | 64.32 | 65.32 |
| EL-2 | 75.07 | 76.07 |
| EL-3 | 82.04 | 83.04 |
| EL-4 | 86.95 | 87.95 |
| EL-5 | 95.13 | 96.13 |
| EL-6 | $103.80 | $104.80 |
| EL-7 | $112.09 | $113.09 |
| EL-8 | $119.50 | $120.50 |
| EL-9 | $127.55 | $128.55 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, can contact us at [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Any employee inquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to IBEW.

Original signed by

Marc Thibodeau A/Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
