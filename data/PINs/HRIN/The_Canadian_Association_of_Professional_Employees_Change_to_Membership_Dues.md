# The Canadian Association of Professional Employees - Change to membership dues

Date: September 25, 2023

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and the Public Service Pay Centre.

The Canadian Association of Professional Employees (CAPE) has informed us of Changes in monthly membership dues effective January 1, 2024.

Details follow:

| Frequency | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly | $57.10 | $48.00 |
| Bi-Weekly | $28.55 | $24.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Employees should direct any questions on this matter to [general@acep-cape.ca](mailto:general@acep-cape.ca).

Original signed by:

Wendy Braithwaite Director, Compensation Policy, Interpretations, and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
