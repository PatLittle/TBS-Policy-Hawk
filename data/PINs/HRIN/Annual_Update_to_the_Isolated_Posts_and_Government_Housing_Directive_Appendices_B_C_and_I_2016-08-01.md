# Annual Update to the Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive - Appendices B, C, and I - August 2016

Date: August 1, 2016

To: Functional Heads of Administration, Human Resources and Financial Service

CC: Chairs and Executive Directors of Northern Federal Regional Councils, Departmental Designated Coordinators for the IPGHD, Committee members of the NJC IPGHD (for information)

Please distribute this notice and the amended appendices of the National Joint Council (NJC) Isolated Posts and Government Housing Directive (IPGHD) as quickly as possible to persons at your headquarters and regional offices who are involved in the administration of the allowances.

The purpose of this notice is to provide you with the approved updates to the NJC IPGHD. The changes in Appendices B, C, and I will be effective August 1, 2016.

These changes were arrived at in accordance with the approved methodologies. The NJC IPGHD has been amended to reflect these changes:

1. The [Environment Allowance rates, Appendix B](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=127&lang=eng), have been adjusted for 2016.
2. The [Living Cost Differential rates, Appendix C](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=128&lang=eng), have been adjusted effective August 1, 2016.

The [Meal or Rations rates Appendix I](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/directive/index.php?sid=134&lang=eng) have been adjusted effective August 1, 2016. Details regarding the methodology can be found in the “[Guide to National Joint Council Rates and Allowances](http://www.njc-cnm.gc.ca/doc.php?did=117%20&lang=en)”.

Any retroactive payments resulting from the above changes are to be administered in the same manner as retroactive increases to employees’ pay.

Any questions from employees and regional staff should be directed to the appropriate [Designated Departmental IPGHD Coordinators](http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/psm-fpfm/pay-remuneration/other-autres/ddhc-cmdl-eng.asp) who, as required, can contact the appropriate officers at the Treasury Board Secretariat.

Claudia Zovatto Senior Director, Union Engagement & NJC Support Compensation and Labour Relations Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
