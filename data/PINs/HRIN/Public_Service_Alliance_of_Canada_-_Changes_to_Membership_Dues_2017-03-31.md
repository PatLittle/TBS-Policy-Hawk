# Public Service Alliance of Canada - Changes to Membership Dues

Date: April 11, 2017

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the members of the National Health Union (NHU) have agreed to merge with the Union of Environment Workers (UEW). The new Component will be named the Union of Health and Environment Workers (UHEW). The merge will be complete at PSAC by May 1st, 2017.

Please note that UHEW’s portion of the union dues rate will decrease from 0.5709% to 0.5% effective May 1st, 2017.

Once this information is updated in the pay system, members will see an adjustment in their dues.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the Public Service Alliance of Canada.

Terry Findlay A/Director Core Public Administration Compensation Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
