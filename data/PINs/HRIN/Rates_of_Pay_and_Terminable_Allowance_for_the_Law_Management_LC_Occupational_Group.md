# Rates of Pay and Terminable Allowance for the Law Management (LC) Occupational Group - December 2014

Date: December 11, 2014

To: Heads of Human Resources; Directors/Chiefs of Labour; Relations and Compensation

I am pleased to announce the revised rates of pay, as approved by the Treasury Board of Canada, for the Law Management (LC) group.

## Rates of Pay

The rates of pay applicable to the Law Management (LC) group can be found online at: [Rates of Pay for Certain Excluded and Unrepresented Employees](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/pay/rates-pay/rates-pay-unrepresented-senior-excluded-employees.html).

The attached [Appendix A](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/information-notice/rates-pay-terminable-allowance-law-management-occupational-group.html#Toc-a) reports the rates of pay for the Law Management (LC) group.

## Terminable Allowance

A terminable allowance has been approved for the Law Management (LC) group levels 1 to 3. The terminable allowance will be effective as of April 1, 2013 and expire on March 31, 2015. An incumbent occupying a Law Management (LC) position performing the duties of the position is eligible for the terminable allowance.

The terminable allowance amounts can be found at [Appendix A](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/information-notice/rates-pay-terminable-allowance-law-management-occupational-group.html#Toc-a).

Note:

Toronto rates of pay will only be used to determine the terminable allowance for employees in the Law Management (LC) group who are located in Toronto and receive Toronto rates of pay.

The terminable allowance will not be included in the calculation of performance pay for eligible employees occupying Law Management (LC) positions.

For employees working less than the normal 37.5 hour work week, the allowance will be prorated based on the number of hours they work compared to a 37.5 hour work week.

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required will direct their questions to the Compensation and Labour Relations group at the following email address: [Contact Interpretations by E-mail : Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).
