# Public Service Alliance of Canada – Changes to Membership Dues

Date: March 1, 2018

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Public Service Alliance of Canada (PSAC) has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues.

Seven (7) of the Components within PSAC will have their membership dues increased or decreased effective March 1, 2018.

The following seven (7) changes are to be made by component:

## Agriculture Union:

The component portion will increase from 0.7159% to 0.7250%.

## Canada Employment and Immigration Union:

The component portion will increase from 0.7303% to 0.7320%.

## Customs and Immigration Union:

The component portion will increase from 0.44967% to 0.44967% + $3.11 /month.

## Government Services Union:

The component portion will increase from 0.5610% to 0.6260%.

## Union of Health and Environment Workers:

The component portion will decrease from 0.5000% to 0.4966%.

## Union of National Employees:

The component portion will decrease from 0.6000% to 0.5956%.

## Union of Safety and Justice Employees:

The component portion will increase from 0.7048% to 0.7203%.

Once this information is updated in the pay system, members will see an adjustment in their dues.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to their respective Component.

Original signed by

Terry Findlay a/Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
