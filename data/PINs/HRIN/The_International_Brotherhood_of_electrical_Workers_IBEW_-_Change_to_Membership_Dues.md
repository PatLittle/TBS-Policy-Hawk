# The International Brotherhood of electrical Workers (IBEW) - Change to Membership Dues

Date: March 31, 2023

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and the Public Service Pay Centre

The International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers has informed us of changes in monthly membership dues effective May 1, 2023.

Details follow:

| Level | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EL-1 | $84.92 | $86.92 |
| EL-2 | $98.24 | $100.24 |
| EL-3 | $106.86 | $108.86 |
| EL-4 | $113.00 | $115.00 |
| EL-5 | $123.14 | $125.14 |
| EL-6 | $133.90 | $135.90 |
| EL-7 | $144.20 | $146.20 |
| EL-8 | $151.42 | $153.42 |
| EL-9 | $163.38 | $165.38 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Employees should direct any questions on this matter to [office@ibew2228.ca](mailto:office@ibew2228.ca).

Original signed by

Wendy Braithwaite Director, Compensation Policy, Interpretations, and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
