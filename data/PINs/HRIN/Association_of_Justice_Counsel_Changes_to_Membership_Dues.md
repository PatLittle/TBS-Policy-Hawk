Date: May 27, 2015

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Association of Justice Counsel has advised us of changes in monthly membership dues effective July 1, 2015 and July 1, 2016.

Membership dues will change as follows:

- July 1, 2015 – the membership dues will increase from 0.75% to 1.0%.
- July 1, 2016 – the membership dues will increase from 1.0% to 1.25%.

These adjustments will be made automatically.

Employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed to the Association of Justice Counsel.

Kevin Marchand Senior Director Core Public Administration Compensation Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
