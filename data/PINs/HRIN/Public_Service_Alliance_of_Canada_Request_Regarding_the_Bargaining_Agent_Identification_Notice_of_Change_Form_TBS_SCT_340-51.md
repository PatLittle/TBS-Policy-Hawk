# Public Service Alliance of Canada - Request Regarding the Bargaining Agent Identification Notice of Change Form TBS/SCT 340-51

Date: July 11, 2013

To: Directors/Heads of Compensation

The purpose of this bulletin is to inform you of a request from the Public Service Alliance of Canada to streamline the use of the Bargaining Agent Identification Notice of Change form TBS/SCT 340-51. Note that this request differs from the instructions provided in our [information bulletin dated September 30, 2010](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/information-notice/modifications-form-bargaining-agent-identification-notice-change.html).

The Public Service Alliance of Canada has made some changes to their internal membership management system. As a result, they have made a request that form TBS/SCT 340-51 not be sent in the following circumstances when the change is:

- To the classification/level/component or local, within the Public Service Alliance of Canada;
- For less than a full calendar month of employment; and
- For an individual who has been hired as a term for a period less than three (3) months.

Please note that this change will both simplify the process for compensation advisors and reduce the number of forms they will have to process and send to this bargaining agent. This new procedure is effective immediately and applies only to members of the Public Service Alliance of Canada.

Please disseminate this information to your compensation advisors for their action, as appropriate.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to the Public Service Alliance of Canada.

Original signed by

Barry Fennessy Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
