# Canadian Association of Professional Employees: Change to Membership Dues

Date: November 21, 2025

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, and Public Service Pay Centre

This information bulletin is to inform you that the Canadian Association of Professional Employees (CAPE) has changed the rate of its monthly membership dues, effective January 1, 2026.

Membership dues are decreasing for all members of CAPE as follows:

| Frequency | Current rate | New rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly | $61.33 | $48.00 |
| Biweekly | $30.66 | $24.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No action is required.

Any employee enquiries regarding these membership dues changes are to be directed by email to [general@acep-cape.ca](mailto:general@acep-cape.ca).

Original signed by

Wendy Bennen Director, Compensation Policy and Pay Administration Employee Relations and Total Compensation Sector Office of the Chief Human Resources Office
