# Membership Dues Changes for the Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO)

Date: March 3, 2011

To: Heads, Compensation; Heads, Labour Relations

The Association of Canadian Financial Officers (ACFO) has advised us of a change to membership dues, taking effect on July 1, 2011.

The ACFO membership dues will increase as follows:

| Classification | Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| All FI members | $45.00 | $50.00 |

These adjustments will be made automatically. No departmental input will be required.

Departmental Compensation and Labour Relations managers should direct any questions to their appropriate corporate officials who, if need be, may contact us at [Contact Interpretations by E-mail Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Any employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to ACFO.

Marc Thibodeau A/Senior Director Compensation Management, Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
