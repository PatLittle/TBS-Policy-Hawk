# The Canadian Merchant Service Guild (CMSG) – Changes to Membership Dues

Date: March 19, 2021

To: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

Subject: The Canadian Merchant Service Guild (CMSG) – Changes to Membership Dues

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform departments that the Canadian Merchant Service Guild (CMSG) has advised us of changes in the monthly membership dues, for Ships’ Officers (SO) effective April 1, 2021.

The monthly membership dues will increase from $105.35 to $107.50.

The monthly membership dues for the Coast Guard College Cadets (SO-MAO-00) will increase from $11.70 to $11.90.

Employee enquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to CMSG, [cmsg@ottawa-email.com](mailto:cmsg@ottawa-email.com).

Original signed by

Angelo Gatto A/Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Employment Conditions and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
