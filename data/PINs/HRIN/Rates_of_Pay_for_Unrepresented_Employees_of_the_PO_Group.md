# Rates of Pay for Unrepresented Employees of the PO Group

**Date:**: February 13, 2018

**To:**: Heads of Human Resources, Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation, Public Service Pay Centre, Miramichi

Please be advised that the Treasury Board of Canada recently approved new rates of pay for unrepresented employees in the Police Operations Support Group (PO).

The attached appendix provides the rates of pay for the unrepresented positions.

The new rates of pay will be available on the TBS Website in the near future.

All questions should be directed to your Departmental Corporate Labour Relations or Corporate Compensation Official who, if required, will direct their questions to the Compensation and Labour Relations group at the following email address: [Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca](mailto:Interpretations@tbs-sct.gc.ca).

Original signed by Zia Proulx Senior Director Compensation and Collective Bargaining Management Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
