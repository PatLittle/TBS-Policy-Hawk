Date: May 10, 2013

To: Directors/Chiefs of Labour Relations and Compensation

The purpose of this information bulletin is to inform you that the Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association has advised us of a change in membership dues effective July 1, 2013.

The monthly membership dues for all chargehand and production supervisor employees located on the East Coast will decrease as follows:

| Current Rate | New Rate |
| --- | --- |
| $75.00 | $50.00 |

This adjustment will be made automatically.

Any employee inquiries regarding this membership dues change are to be directed to the Federal Government Dockyard Chargehands Association.

Questions regarding this bulletin should be directed to your departmental Corporate Compensation official.

Barry Fennessy Senior Director Compensation Management Core Public Administration Compensation and Labour Relations Office of the Chief Human Resources Officer
