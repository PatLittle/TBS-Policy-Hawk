# Contracting Policy Notice 2025-2: Rescission of section D.5, “COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel,” from the Directive on the Management of Procurement

Date: January 13, 2025

To: Senior Designated Officials for the Management of Procurement and Functional Heads of Procurement, Administration and Finance units at all departments

Subject: Rescission of section D.5 from the Directive on the Management of Procurement

## Overview

On October 4, 2024, the Government of Canada rescinded the previously suspended Policy on COVID-19 Vaccination for the Core Public Administration Including the Royal Canadian Mounted Police.

To align with this change, the [Directive on the Management of Procurement](https://www.tbs-sct.canada.ca/pol/doc-eng.aspx?id=32692) is amended effective December 19, 2024, to remove section D.5, “COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel.” As the requirements in section D.5 were previously suspended on June 20, 2022, there is no change to departments.

Previous [Contracting Policy Notice 2021-4](https://web.archive.org/web/20240625060026/https:/www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/policy-notice/contracting-policy-notice-2021-4-amendments-appendix-d-directive-management-procurement-related-covid-19-vaccination-supplier-personnel.html) and [Contracting Policy Notice 2022-2](https://web.archive.org/web/20240625055555/https:/www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/policy-notice/contracting-policy-notice-2022-2.html) relating to section D.5 of the directive are archived and are no longer in effect.

## Enquiries

Questions about this policy notice should be directed to TBS [Public Enquiries](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/corporate/contact.html#enquiries).
